Chocolate Chip Cake - My favourite recipe

Let's get down to business! Roll up your sleeves because here we go!

First of all, for preparing this meal, you'll need:

  • 200 g Chocolate
  • 4 eggs
  • 400 g Flour
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • 2 tsp Vegetable Oil

To make it look and taste great, just follow these simple steps:

  1. Cut chocolate in small chips 🍫
  2. Beat eggs 🥚
    • Break eggs
    • Separate eggwhite from yolk
    • Beat the eggwhites
  3. Add sugar to eggwhites 🥣
  4. Stir well 🥣
  5. Add everything else except chocolate crisps 🥣
  6. When the mixture is evenly stirred, pour it in the baking pan
  7. Add chocolate crisps 🍫
  8. Bake for 20 minutes on 230 degrees Celsius 🔥
  9. Cut, decorate and serve 🍰
